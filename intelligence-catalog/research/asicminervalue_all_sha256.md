## Data Notes

- All specs sourced from [asicminervalue.com](https://www.asicminervalue.com) embedded data (Next.js page JSON)
- The efficiency page lists currently-relevant miners; older/less efficient models are sourced from manufacturer-specific pages
- Efficiency (J/TH) = Power (W) ÷ Hashrate (TH/s); calculated directly from spec values
- Cooling variants (Air / Hydro / Immersion) are listed as separate entries where specs differ

- Models marked with variant suffixes (+, Pro, XP, j, e, etc.) have meaningfully different specs
