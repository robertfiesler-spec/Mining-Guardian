# MicroBT Whatsminer — Complete SHA-256 Variant List

All known MicroBT Whatsminer Bitcoin (SHA-256) ASIC miner variants, compiled from multiple sources including the official Whatsminer shop, Hashrate Index, ASIC Miner Value, D-Central, ASIC Marketplace, CryptoMinerBros, BT-Miners, Apexto Mining, Zeus Mining, and MiningNow.

> **Note on hashrates:** Many models ship in batches with slightly varying hashrates (e.g. 390–420 TH/s). The "Stock Hashrate" column lists the nominal/rated value at stock settings (25°C). "Power" is the nominal wall-power at stock settings. Efficiency is derived from those two figures. Where multiple batch variants exist (e.g. M50 114T vs 118T vs 120T), the nominal advertised value is used.

## Notes on Naming Convention

- **Air-cooled**: Base models (e.g. M60, M61) and S/S+/S++ suffixes indicate higher chip bins (S = standard tier, S+ = enhanced, S++ = premium cherry-picked chips)
- **Hydro-cooled**: Odd tens digit in the third position (M_3_ and M_7_ in hydro context: M63, M73, M79, M7D) — these require water-loop infrastructure at 380–480 V 3-phase
- **Immersion-cooled**: Middle digit 6 (M56, M66, M76, M78) — these sit submerged in dielectric fluid tanks at 380–480 V
- **M64 exception**: Despite having a 4 in the middle, the M64 is a hydro variant designed for home heating at single-phase 220–277 V
- **M6DS vs M63**: The M6DS series is the successor to M63, using the same 4U form factor but with next-gen chip generation (Gen 7 chip in a Gen 6-form factor chassis)
